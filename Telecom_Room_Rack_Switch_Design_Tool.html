Key Formulas
connectedLoad = Σ (deviceCount × devicePower) designLoad = connectedLoad × (1 + cableLoss) × (1 + spareMargin) switchesByPower = ⌈ designLoad / switchBudget ⌉ switchesByPorts = ⌈ totalDevices×(1+sparePorts) / portsPerSwitch ⌉ switchesRequired= max(switchesByPower, switchesByPorts, 1) worstPortDemand = max(devicePower_i) × (1 + cableLoss) totalU_needed = Σ (item height in U) rackSize = nextStandardSize(totalU_needed × (1+uSpare)) requiredUPS_VA = totalPowerLoad_W / powerFactor heatLoad_BTU/hr = totalPowerLoad_W × 3.412
