📐 Calculation Method
bitrate = MP × fps × codecFactor × sceneFactor + audio
effective = bitrate × recordingFactor
GB/day = effective × 10.8
storage = GB/day × days × (1 + overhead) / 1000

Codec factors: H.264=0.070 · H.265=0.045 · H.265+=0.032
Scene factors: Low=0.85 · Med=1.00 · High=1.20 · VHigh=1.40
Recording: Continuous=1.0 · Motion=activity% · Smart=max(8%,activity×0.75)

RAID overhead: RAID1=50% · RAID5=1/n drives · RAID6=2/n drives · RAID10=50%
Network: 1Gbps Ethernet ≈ 500 Mbps usable · 100Mbps ≈ 55-60 Mbps usable
